About Me

I am a passionate plant pathologist specializing in bacterial pathogens, with expertise in Xanthomonas species. My work spans bioinformatics, molecular phylogeny, and plant-microbe interactions. I currently manage a pharmacy while consulting in research and translation, supporting students and researchers with bioinformatics analyses, scientific writing, and translations for international journals.
